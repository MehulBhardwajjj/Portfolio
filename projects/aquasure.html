AquaSure – HMPI Analyzer

AquaSure is a scientific tool designed to calculate the Heavy Metal Pollution Index (HMPI) in water samples. The platform helps researchers, environmental scientists, and policymakers analyze water quality by processing heavy metal concentration data and producing pollution index scores.

Problem

Water pollution caused by heavy metals poses serious risks to ecosystems and human health. Researchers and policymakers require reliable analytical tools to measure contamination levels and assess environmental safety. However, processing and interpreting large datasets of heavy metal concentrations can be complex and time-consuming.

Solution

AquaSure simplifies environmental data analysis by computing the Heavy Metal Pollution Index (HMPI) automatically. The system processes concentration values for various heavy metals and generates meaningful pollution scores, allowing researchers to quickly evaluate water quality and make informed decisions.

Tech Stack

  • Python
  • React
  • Scientific Computing
  • Environmental Data Analysis

Architecture

The platform follows a simple data-analysis architecture where environmental data is collected, processed through HMPI calculations, and visualized for analysis.

  • Data Input Layer – heavy metal concentration data
  • Processing Layer – HMPI computation
  • Analysis Layer – environmental risk evaluation
  • Visualization Layer – results presented to researchers
